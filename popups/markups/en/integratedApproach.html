Main seminar topics
  1. Introduction to the integrative approach. Possibilities of integration on the micro and macro levels
  2. General model of psychotherapy as one of the forms of integrative approaches.
  3. Empirically supported model for professional development of a psychotherapist.
  4. Research in psychotherapy: from case analysis to meta-analysis. Methodology and practicum.
  5. Submersion into the theory and methodology of leading psychotherapy modalities: psychoanalytical approach, humanistic approach (from procedural to the focused and structural models), cognitive-behavioral psychotherapy, family systemic psychotherapy.
  6. Possibility for integration on creative and practical levels (both in group and individual formats).
